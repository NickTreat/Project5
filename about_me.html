About Me

My Name is Nick Treat and I have been working for Amazon since 2020. I live in Washington State, where when I'm not at work scanning laptops, you can find me hiking the many trails and mountains or looking for fancy rocks on the beach. Reading, gaming, and learning are some of my hobbies, as well as eating too much good food, and watching far too much Youtube.

This page will be about me and my Software Development Journey. I've usually always had more manual labor type jobs, jobs that require mostly physical work, but since starting at Amazon and being exposed to all the different types of IT jobs that exist, and at the urging from family members who already went and learned coding, I figured there's no better time than now to start learning the skills I'll need to eventually get a job that let's me use my mind more than my body.
